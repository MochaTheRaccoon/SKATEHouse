AN INTERVIEW

What made you get into skateboarding?

the skating community is like a safe space for me, whenever i feel sad or garbage or anything like that i'd usually just go out

to the nearest chill spot and practice some tricks while calming down about everything happening to me

so going out and hanging out with likeminded people is a really cool thing for me

What inspired you to start skateboarding?

it actually looked fun, so i wanted to give it a go
